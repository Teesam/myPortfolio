The very first technology you'll most likely get your hands on if you're looking to get onto web dev. The magic HTML 5 produces is profounding, giving you an alomost seamless effort in populating the web.

Have you ever taken the time out to wonder about whatever it is that's responsible for making the web pages you visit every other day look so beautiful? Puzzle no more. CSS does so much under the hood than is given credit for. HTML 5 + CSS = beautiful.

Into the world of programming language. This guy here called javascript, is the what you'd call the king of frontend.
